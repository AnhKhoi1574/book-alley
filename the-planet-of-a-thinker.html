Description

The Planet of a Thinker is a semi-autobiographical book about the author's journey of self-discovery. The book tells the story of her thoughts, feelings, and experiences as she grows from a naive and optimistic young girl into a more mature and understanding woman.

The Planet of a Thinker is an honest, insightful, and emotional book. It has touched the hearts of many readers and helped them to understand themselves and life better.
