Privacy Policy for Book Alley

1. Information We Collect:

Personal Information: We may collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, shipping address, and payment details when you create an account, place an order, or interact with our services.

Non-Personal Information: We may also gather non-personal information, such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and browsing behavior, through cookies and similar technologies.

2. How We Use Your Information:

Order Processing: We use your personal information to process and fulfill your orders, including shipping, delivery, and customer support.

Account Management: Your account information helps us manage your preferences, purchases, and personalized experiences.

Communication: We may use your contact details to send you order updates, newsletters, promotions, and important announcements related to Book Alley. You can opt-out of marketing communications at any time.

Analytics and Improvements: Non-personal information helps us analyze website traffic, user behavior, and trends, allowing us to improve our website's performance and user experience.

3. Information Sharing:

Third-Party Service Providers: We may share your personal information with trusted third-party service providers to facilitate order processing, payment processing, shipping, and other essential functions.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation.

Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of Book Alley, your information may be transferred as part of the business assets.

4. Data Security:

We implement security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. However, please note that no data transmission over the internet or storage method is entirely secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies:

Book Alley uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, track website usage, and improve services. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Children's Privacy:

Book Alley does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information on our website, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Choices:

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information stored by Book Alley. To exercise these rights or inquire about our Privacy Policy, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy:

Book Alley may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be notified on our website, and the revised policy will be effective upon posting.
