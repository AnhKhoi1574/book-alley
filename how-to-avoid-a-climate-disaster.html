Description

How to Avoid a Climate Disaster is a book by Bill Gates about the climate crisis. The book argues that climate change is a serious threat to humanity, but that it is not too late to avert disaster. Gates discusses the different solutions that are available to us, and argues that we need to invest in a variety of technologies in order to achieve net-zero emissions.

The book is written in a clear and concise style, and it is packed with valuable information. It is an essential read for anyone who wants to understand the climate crisis and what we can do to address it.
