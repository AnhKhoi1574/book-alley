Give Me a Ticket to Childhood

400.000₫

Author: Nguyen Nhat Anh


Description

Give Me a Ticket to Childhood is a collection of short stories that captures the essence of childhood. The stories are about the simple joys of childhood, such as playing with friends, exploring the neighborhood, and learning new things. They are also about the challenges of childhood, such as dealing with bullies, making mistakes, and growing up.

The stories are told from the perspective of a child, and they are full of humor, warmth, and nostalgia. They will transport readers back to their own childhoods, and they will make them appreciate the simple joys of life.
